Travel Tips

Budget Wisely

Always add a 15-20% buffer to your budget for unexpected expenses that may arise during your trip.

Accommodation Savings

Consider staying in hostels, guesthouses, or using services like Airbnb to save on accommodation costs.

Flight Deals

Use flight comparison tools and set up price alerts to catch the best deals. Booking 2-3 months in advance often yields the best prices.

Food Expenses

Eat like a local! Street food and local restaurants are not only more authentic but usually much cheaper than tourist spots.

Currency Exchange

Avoid exchanging currency at airports or hotels. Use local ATMs or exchange offices in the city center for better rates.

Local Transport

Research public transportation options before your trip. Many cities offer tourist passes that can save you money on multiple rides.
